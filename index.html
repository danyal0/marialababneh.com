About

Research

I study stress and depression in veteran populations, exploring prevention and intervention strategies to improve mental health and resilience. My methods include mixed-methods research, community-engaged approaches, and implementation science to translate evidence into practice.

Current goals:

  • Identify risk and protective factors for veteran depression
  • Design scalable interventions to reduce stress symptoms
  • Partner with clinics and community organizations for implementation
